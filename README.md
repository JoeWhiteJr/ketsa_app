# Ketsa

**The all-in-one privacy platform that hardens your phone, documents your encounters, and teaches you your rights.**

> *Knowing and exercising your rights isn't paranoia — it's preparation.*

## What is Ketsa?

Ketsa is a mobile privacy protection platform designed to give users real-time control over their digital and physical safety. It hardens your device, provides encounter documentation tools, and delivers rights-based education — all in one app.

The name evokes the **kestrel** — the hovering falcon known for holding perfectly still while watching the ground below.
